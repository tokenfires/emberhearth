## Why This Matters for EmberHearth

Directly relevant to EmberHearth's autonomous operation mode. Shows that step-wise reasoning creates greedy policies that fail over long horizons. FLARE (Future-aware Lookahead with Reward Estimation) enables LLaMA-8B to outperform GPT-4o — critical for task planning.

## Key Findings

- Step-wise reasoning induces greedy policies that fail for long-horizon planning
- Locally optimal choices create early myopic commitments amplified over time
- Introduces FLARE: Future-aware Lookahead with Reward Estimation
- LLaMA-8B with FLARE frequently outperforms GPT-4o with standard step-by-step reasoning
- Establishes clear distinction between reasoning and planning capabilities
